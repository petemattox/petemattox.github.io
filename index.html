Experience

I've worked a lot of different jobs. I've been a dishwasher, movie theatre projectionist, I've delivered pizzas, delivered kayaks, done industrial laundry, worked as backcountry hut crew for the Appalachian Mountain Club, been an Outward Bound instructor, baker, ranch hand, substitute teacher, English teacher, house painter, FedEx seasonal delivery driver, I've loaded UPS trucks, taught SAT classes, and worked as a bartender.

When I was a junior in college, which is also when I was twenty-six, I won a scholarship to spend a year in China. From that point on, China has been a huge part of my life. I lived there five years, learned Mandarin, and have traveled to every province in the country except Tibet. Most recently, I have been a graduate student enrolled concurrently in a couple different programs. One is history (with a focus on East Asia); the other is web map programming.
